Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Bash Adventures is a premier tour operator specializing in unforgettable experiences across Southern Africa. What began as a small family-run business has grown into a trusted name in adventure tourism, with operations in Botswana, South Africa, and Zimbabwe.

Our Mission

To create authentic, sustainable travel experiences that connect visitors with the breathtaking landscapes, rich cultures, and incredible wildlife of Southern Africa while supporting local communities.

Our Vision

To be the most respected adventure travel company in Africa, known for exceptional service, innovative itineraries, and meaningful contributions to conservation efforts.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate travel experts, knowledgeable local guides, and dedicated support staff who all share a deep love for Africa. With an average of 10+ years of experience in the industry, we bring unparalleled expertise to every journey we plan.
